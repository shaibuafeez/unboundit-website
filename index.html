Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Screen recording and video editing without the bloat. Every feature earns its place.

Screen Recording

Capture your screen, camera, and microphone. System audio included on macOS.

Auto Zoom

Automatic click-driven zoom that follows your cursor. Configurable depth, easing, and timing.

Cursor Effects

Smoothing, motion blur, click bounce, sway, and custom cursor styles. Your mouse, cinematic.

Backgrounds & Frames

Wallpapers, rounded corners, padding, shadows, and blur. Make every recording look polished.

Captions

AI-powered auto captions via Whisper. Multi-language support with full style customization.

Fast Export

Hardware-accelerated encoding. MP4 and GIF output with quality presets. WebCodecs pipeline.

AI Pipeline

Your voice, any language.

Record once. AI clones your voice, translates your content, and re-renders your face to match the new audio.

Voice cloning — extract your voice from any recording and clone it for dubbing
AI translation — translate captions to 10+ languages with one click
Dubbed audio — generate speech in the target language using your cloned voice
Lip sync — Wav2Lip re-renders your face to match the dubbed audio
